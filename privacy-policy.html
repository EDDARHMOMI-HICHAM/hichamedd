4. Cookies and Google Analytics

To assess audience behavior patterns, this platform implements tracking scripts provided by Google Analytics. These performance tools rely on **Cookies**—small indicator files saved directly inside your local web browser storage.

The dataset generated via cookies regarding homepage usage is safely transmitted straight onto dedicated Google server infrastructures. You can proactively prevent cookies from executing by altering tracking parameters right inside your specific browser preferences.
